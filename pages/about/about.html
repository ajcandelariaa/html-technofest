Technofest

The TECHNOFEST 2022 is a convention that celebrates the bounty of technology that this generation has to offer to its people. Esteemed guests and speakers are invited to talk for Students of FEU Tech, Diliman, and Alabang, as well as from different colleges and universities, on matters like challenges and technology fallbacks. Delegates from other colleges and universities could also join competitions and paper presentations related to their degree and program. This program will celebrate technology by boosting its participants’ enthusiasm that shall trigger creativity and innovation that shall make a mark for centuries to come.

FEU INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

FEU Tech offers competitive and innovative academic programs supplemented with strong academic and industry partnerships that provide its students opportunities to enhance their education. Students undergo an Internship Program that provides intensive, real-world training with its industry partners-a network of about 800 technology and engineering corporations nationwide. FEU Tech also offers Study Abroad and Internship Abroad Programs that give students a chance to learn and immerse in multicultural environments and cultures with partner schools in Taiwan and Korea.

FEU ALABANG

As one of the pioneering academic institutions in the area, FEU Alabang is the continuing realization of its founder’s, Dr. Nicanor Reyes Sr., aspiration that all graduates of the FEU community will build rewarding careers in professions that will be beneficial to the country’s growth.

FEU DILIMAN

FEU Diliman was established in 1994 to commemorate the birth centennial of Nicanor Reyes Sr., the founder and the first president of FEU. It is situated in a ten-hectare property in Mapayapa Village, Barangay Pasong Tamo, Quezon City (formerly categorized in Barangay Diliman, Quezon City) and provides modern facilities for its Accountancy, Business, I.T., High School, Grade School, and Kindergarten students.

The integrated schools of FEU Alabang, FEU Diliman, and FEU Institute of Technology are a vibrant community of technology scholars and professionals seeking to serve the Nation, the Industry, and the World. Proudly providing the ultimate academic experience. Their rich, vibrant community allows students to make life-long friends, have unforgettable moments, get real-world training, and enjoy the ideal campus life.
